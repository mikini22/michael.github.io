Advanced Display Solutions

Advanced Displays is an electronic display platform that can be used for advertising, building brand awareness, and delivering important information about a company’s products and services via high-quality graphics and videos. By 2020, experts predict this particular advertising medium will transform into an industry that’s worth more than $20 billion. Since digital signage displays can deliver comprehensive announcements, timetables, emergency information, and product descriptions in real-time, they can give businesses an edge over their competition. In a day and age where consumers no longer respond to print ads and the cost of client acquisition is becoming more expensive.

Types of Advanced Display Solutions

Video wall

A video wall is a special multi-monitor setup that consists of multiple computer monitors, video projectors, or television sets tiled together contiguously or overlapped in order to form one large screen.

Modular LED Walls

It's one of the components of a finished LED display that comprises PCB, IC, LEDs, cables, connectors, resistors, screws, and capacitors. The LED display module integrates all electronics to produce images and videos.If you want a high-impact display for a project, the modular LED display panels are a good fit. You can configure them the way you want for a seamless viewing experience.

Interactive Display

An interactive screen is a computer driven device, allowing users to access and manipulate electronic files by means of a LCD display. The interactive LCD will be connected to a computer with USB, and the computer then connected back to the LCD with a video cable.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Digital Signage

Digital signage, sometimes called electronic signage, refers to display technologies like LED walls (or video walls), projection and LCD monitors to vividly display webpages, videos, directions, restaurant menus, marketing messages or digital images.
